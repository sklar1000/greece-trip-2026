# Original Itinerary — Paros & Milos

[Back to home](./) | [Planning Guide](planning-guide) | [Island Research](island-research)

This is the original proposed plan, captured as-is for reference.

---

## Overview

- **Dates**: June 1-10, 2026
- **Route**: Newark → Athens → Paros (4 nights) → Milos (3 nights) → Athens (1 night) → Newark
- **Islands**: Paros + Milos

---

## June 1 — Fly to Athens

- United Flight UA124 Direct from Newark to Athens 3:50 PM - 8:10 AM
- $680 for Economy (as of time of planning)

---

## PAROS (June 2-6)

### June 2 — Travel Day
- Arrive Athens in the morning
- Fly Athens to Paros: Aegean Air OA066, 11:15 AM - 12:05 PM
- $162 USD

### Accommodation
- **Harmony Villa** (VRBO)
- **$428 per person** for 4 nights
- Located in **Naousa** — most central/in-town area
- Walk to the beach and town, private pool

### Day-by-Day (Hypothetical)

| Day | Plan |
|-----|------|
| June 3 | Beach day at Cabana |
| June 4 | Day trip to Antiparos — shop, eat at Pippinos, lounge at Beach House |
| June 5 | Boat day |

### Things to Do in Paros
1. Beach day at Cabana
2. Fresh juices, coffee and baked goods at Ragoussis
3. Sunset dinner at Siparos
4. Day trip to Antiparos (15 min ferry ride) — shop, eat at Pippinos, lounge at Beach House
5. Visit historic town of Lefkes
6. Dine and dance at Barbarossa in Naoussa
7. Explore the island by boat
8. Lunch spots: Comeback, Taverna Gladkos, Allas Souvlaki

---

## MILOS (June 6-9)

### June 6 — Travel Day
- Ferry on SeaJets 6:10 PM - 7:55 PM
- ~$70 USD per person

### Accommodation
- **Aeolis Hotel** in Adamantas
- Can accommodate group size on the dates
- **$85 per person** (for the stay)
- Adamantas recommended for accessibility, nightlife, and bus to beaches

### Things to Do in Milos
1. Kleftiko Sailing Trip
2. Check out the village of Fourkovouni
3. Discover Tsigrado beach
4. Lunch at Medusa
5. Dinner and Sunset at Plaka
6. Swim in caves
7. Eat seafood at Astakas
8. Day trip to Kimolos island
9. Akri Bar for Cocktails
10. Go to moon beach (Sarakiniko)

---

## June 9 — Fly to Athens
- Fly Milos to Athens: Aegean Air, 2:30 PM - 3:10 PM
- $155 USD
- Stay the night in Athens (Plaka area, hotel TBD)
- Dinner suggestion: Ermou 18 Rooftop

## June 10 — Fly Home
- United Flight UA125 Athens to Newark, 10:45 AM - 2:05 PM
- $1,484 for Economy (as of time of planning)
